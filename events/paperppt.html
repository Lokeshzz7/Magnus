Rules and Regulations

1.Paper Presentation

-> A maximum of 3 members per paper is allowed.

-> The topic for the presentation can be chosen by your own.

-> Each team will get 10 minutes for presentation and followed by the question & answer session.

-> Presenters must bring their laptops or pen drives to present their paper on the day of event.

-> Kindly bring your ID card and receipt on the event day.

-> People from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person cannot be a part of multiple teams for the same event.

-> Paper should be in IEEE format.

-> Plagiarism is not entertained.

-> Soft copies of the paper must be submitted by the participants.

-> The decision of judges will be final and no arguments will be entertained.
